Our Story

Our story began in November 2009 in Dublin, Ireland. We crossed paths at a hostel where we were both staying. Julie was backpacking and Marcel was traveling abroad and improving his English. We both shared a passion for travel and enjoyed the country's spectacular sites and all the beauty it had to offer. After exploring it for 5 days together, Julie continued her journey to France and we remained friends via facebook.

Almost a year later, Marcel moved back to SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil and Julie shortly after became a flight attendant.

We kept in touch over the years and connected for special occasions, hoping we'd meet again. But we never managed to make it happen.... Until New Years eve 2014! At this point, Marcel is living in Munich, Germany and had contacted Julie to wish her a Happy New Year! Marcel made it his New Years resolution to visit her and Canada.

He arrived in April 2014, met Julie's family and friends, visited the tourist attractions and got caught up.

Shorlty after we began to date long distance and made the best of it as we both enjoy to travel! On one of our trips, Marcel got down on one knee and proposed to Julie in Beirut, Lebanon in September 2015!

And the rest is history :)
