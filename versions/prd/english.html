Our Story

Dublin, Ireland is where it all began. It was November 2009 and we crossed paths at a hostel. Julie was backpacking and Marcel was traveling abroad and improving his English. We both shared a passion for travel and after 5 days of exploring the country's spectacular sites and all its beauty, Julie continued her journey to France and remained friends.

One year later, Marcel moved back to São Paulo, Brazil, while Julie pursued her career as a flight attendant. We kept in touch over the years and connected for special occasions, hoping our paths would cross again. Despite our efforts to meet, we never managed to make it happen. Until New Years Eve 2014, that's when everything changed.

At this point, Marcel was living in Munich, Germany and traveled to Brazil to spend the holidays with his family. On New Years Eve, at the strike of midnight, Marcel made his New Years resolution to visit Julie in Canada. It was at that very moment when Marcel messaged Julie wishing her a Happy New Year and shared his New Years resolution.

In April 2014, Marcel landed in Toronto and met Julie's family and friends. We visited many tourist attractions and got caught up over the 5 years that had passed.

Shortly after, we began to date - thank goodness for Viber, WhatsApp and Facebook! We made the best of it as we both enjoy to travel and began to rendez-vous monthly.

During a family trip in Beirut, Lebanon in September 2015, Marcel got down on one knee during a boat ride at the Raouché and proposed to Julie...and the rest is history.
